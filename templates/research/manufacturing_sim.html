Manufacturing Simulation

A manufacturing simulation web application was developed under the supervision of Dr. Faisal Aqlan at Penn State Behrend. The application was meant as a platform for testing different types of manufacturing workflows and theories (e.g., LEAN manufacturing) in a simulated environment which could provide detailed telemetry. In this simulator, participants build toy cars using building bricks according to customer orders.

Take it for a spin at mfgsim.saamankhali.net.
